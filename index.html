Rama Navami, the celebration of Lord Rama's birth, embodies the essence of cherished values across cultures. The recent completion of the Shri Ram Mandir at His birthplace is a testament to devotees' unwavering dedication. His ideals of righteousness and morality resonate more than ever. ISKCON's distribution of prasadam in Ayodhya, serving four lakh plates over 30 days, exemplifies the spirit of seva. This effort was recognized by the Uttar Pradesh government, further highlighting the significance of this milestone.





Sri Rama NAVAMI Mahostavam

Let us welcome Lord Ram into our lives by drawing inspiration from His ideal behavior and imbibing His exemplary qualities. ISKCON Vijayawada will celebrate Shri Ram Navami on April 16 and 17, 2024, and will also organize Maha Annadaan from April 13 to April 17, 2024.

Annadhan Seva

Shri Ram Navami, the auspicious occasion of Lord Ramachandra's birthday, is an ideal opportunity to please the Lord through service. Distributing prasadam, the mercy of Lord Ramachandra, to every temple visitor is a crucial service during Shri Ram Navami and greatly pleases the Lord. We plan to provide a full plate of prasadam consisting of puri, subji, rice, dahl, and halwa to every participant, with each plate costing Rs. 50/-.

Your modest act of charity has the power to make a difference and bring joy to the faces of those in need.
